CLASS	-		CoolJ.OSShell
METHOD	-
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
				GetCursorPos	|	获取当前的坐标信息
								|	Param：null
								|	Return：pt.x『横坐标』、pt.y『纵坐标』
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
				GetKeyState		|	获取当前的按键状态
								|	Param：null
								|	Return：keyState『按键状态』
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------								
				GetWorkArea		|	获取工作区域的大小Rect
								|	Param：null
								|	Return：rc.left、rc.top、rc.right、rc.bottom
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
				GetScreenArea	|	获取屏幕区域的大小Rect
								|	Param：null
								|	Return：rc.left、rc.top、rc.right、rc.bottom
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
				OpenUrl			|	之前调用默认浏览器打开对应的url
								|	Param：url『访问的web地址』
								|	Return：null
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
				UUID			|	生成所需的GUID
								|	Param：null
								|	Return：GUID
